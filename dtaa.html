DTAA

Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement guidance

Overview

DTAA - In a DTAA signed by India with another country, a specific rate is fixed at which tax has to be deducted from the income paid to the residents of that country. If an NRI earns income in India, the income would be taxed at the rate fixed in the DTAA with the country in which the NRI is based now.
