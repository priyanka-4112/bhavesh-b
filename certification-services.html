Assurance & certification services

Overview

As a Chartered Accountant, I provide reliable and authentic certification services to help individuals, businesses, and organizations meet their statutory and financial requirements. Certification involves verifying and attesting various financial documents, statements, and declarations to ensure their accuracy, transparency, and compliance with applicable laws. I issue certificates such as Net Worth Certificates, Turnover Certificates, Form 15CB for foreign remittances, Projected Financial Statements, and Utilization Certificates, among others. Each certification is carried out with utmost diligence and integrity, ensuring that clients can confidently present their financial information to banks, government authorities, and other stakeholders..
